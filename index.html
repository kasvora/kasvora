Overview

A private investment and strategic holdings firm focused on long-term value creation through capital, structure, and execution.

The firm acquires, invests in, and operates businesses across sectors where capital, structure, and strategic execution can create long-term value. Kasvora’s activities include principal investments in publicly listed and private companies, structured capital solutions, investment banking, and energy-related transactions, with a particular focus on crude oil trading and strategic industrial opportunities.

The firm pursues both minority and control positions and works actively alongside management teams, counterparties, and operating partners to execute complex transactions and long-term growth strategies.
